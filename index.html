sullu chi vadapav wali recipe

✹Ingredients✹

✹For pav✹

  • 1 vada
  • 1ltr pav
  • 1/2kg butter
  • gas
  • cylinder
  • oil

✹For filling✹

  • aadhi cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 tablespoon ground ciniman
  • 1/4 cup butter

✹Instructions✹

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast.
  2. melt the butter and add mixture.
  3. Add the flour and mix until combine into a dough.
  4. kneed the dough for 10 minutes.
